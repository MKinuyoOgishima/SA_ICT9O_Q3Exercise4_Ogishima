Manila Zoo

The Manila Zoo has about 1,000 animals of 90 species. Exotic attractions that bring the zoo to life include Bengal tigers, Malayan civets, lizards and hippos. Expected features include a new zoo, several parks, entertainment and dining options, a museum and a veterinary clinic.This zoo will soon be the center of a festival dedicated to the rich and diverse flora and fauna of the Philippines!
